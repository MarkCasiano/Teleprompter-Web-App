# 🎥 PrompterCam: Mobile Video Teleprompter

PrompterCam is a lightweight, privacy-focused, web-based teleprompter that allows you to record high-quality video while reading a scrolling script. Designed specifically for mobile browsers, it eliminates the need for expensive teleprompter hardware or paid apps.

## ✨ Features

* **Dual-View Interface:** View your scrolling script on the top half and your camera preview on the bottom.
* **Mirror Mode:** Flip text horizontally for use with physical teleprompter glass reflectors.
* **Focus Zone:** A highlighted "reading lane" to keep your eye line consistent and professional.
* **Customizable Controls:** * Adjust **Scroll Speed** to match your speaking pace.
    * Adjust **Font Size** for readability at different distances.
* **Countdown Timer:** A 3-second lead-in to help you compose yourself before recording starts.
* **Script Management:** Save your scripts as `.txt` files to your device or load existing ones instantly.
* **Privacy First:** All processing happens in your browser. No video or text data is ever sent to a server.

## 🚀 Quick Start

1.  **Open the App:** Navigate to your hosted GitHub Pages URL.
2.  **Paste Your Script:** Enter or load your text into the script box.
3.  **Adjust Settings:** Set your preferred font size and scroll speed.
4.  **Position Your Phone:** For the best results, place your phone at eye level.
5.  **Record:** Hit **Start Recording**, wait for the countdown, and read!
6.  **Save:** Once you hit **Stop**, the video will automatically download to your device.

## 🛠️ Built With

* **HTML5 / CSS3**: Responsive, mobile-first design.
* **JavaScript (Vanilla)**: High-performance scrolling and MediaRecorder API for video capture.

## 📱 Mobile Installation

For the best experience, add this to your home screen:
* **iOS (Safari):** Tap the 'Share' icon -> 'Add to Home Screen'.
* **Android (Chrome):** Tap the three-dot menu -> 'Install app' or 'Add to home screen'.
